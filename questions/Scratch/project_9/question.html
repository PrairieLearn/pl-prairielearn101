Your assignment is to watch Events and create a Scratch animation in Scratch. Your scene should include:

  • At least three sprites.
  • At least three backdrops (or stages).
  • At least one broadcast.
  • At least one clone.
  • At least one custom block.
  • At least one variable.
  • At least one loop.
  • At least one condition.
  • At least one event.

Once you have created your scene, select "File" > "Save to your computer". If prompted by your computer to Open or Save the file, be sure to Save it.

Once you have saved your project, rename it to "scratch9.sb3", and upload it here.
