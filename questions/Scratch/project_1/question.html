Your assignment is to watch Sprites and create a scene in Scratch. Your scene should include:

  • At least one backdrop (in addition to the default blank one).
  • At least three different sprites (this may include the default cat).

Once you have created your scene, select "File" > "Save to your computer". If prompted by your computer to Open or Save the file, be sure to Save it.

Once you have saved your project, rename it to "scratch1.sb3", and upload it here.
