Comment Sequence numbers are incremented as follows, depending on the type of segment that is sent. The one that applies to this example is marked with a check:

  • SYN: sender increments its sequence number by 1. {{#params.S}} ✅ {{/params.S}}
  • FIN: sender increments its sequence number by 1. {{#params.F}} ✅ {{/params.F}}
  • Pure ACK (no SYN or FIN, no data payload): sender does not change its sequence number. {{#params.A}} ✅ {{/params.A}}
  • Data segment: sender increments its sequence number by the size of the data payload. {{#params.D}} ✅ {{/params.D}}
