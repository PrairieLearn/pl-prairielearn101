A host is connected to a shared medium that uses CSMA/CA (without RTS/CTS) as its MAC protocol. Suppose {{params.desc.situation}}. What will it do next?

 Wait for the end of the current transmission plus one DIFS, then begin the random backoff procedure. Transmit data. Wait until it receives an ACK or there is an ACK timeout. Freeze the backoff timer until the transmission ends, then resume the backoff when the channel is idle again for one DIFS. Sense the medium for one SIFS. Consider the frame lost, enter random backoff and then re-attempt transmission. Give up. Continue the random backoff procedure, but without increasing the contention window in subsequent iterations. The frame transmission is complete, so the host does not need to do anything else.
